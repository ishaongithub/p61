My Favorite Hobby
Playing the Piano

My hobby is playing the piano. The piano is a very easy instrument to start playing, and although it requires effort to play complex pieces, easier ones can be done by anyone. Whenever I have nothing to do, my first instinct is to go to the piano. Piano helps improve hand-eye coordination, since you would have to be ablle to read notes and play them simuntaneously. It is one of the easiest instruments to play, and a great way to while away time and relax. Playing the instrument may not benefit you in certain ways, like helpng you make money, unless you want to carry it a step further. But it does make you a better person. Playing it can remove any stress and make your day, make you happy.
